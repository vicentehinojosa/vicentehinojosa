# Vicente Hinojosa

**Founder, Institute for Process-Centered Accountability (IPCA)**  
Independent Researcher · Classical Charcoal Artist · Builder

---

I study what generative AI does to the relationship between maker and artifact.

For forty thousand years, the trace between a mark and its maker was never absent. It went unnamed because it never needed a name. Now it does.

That observation is the foundation of **Process-Centered Accountability** — a framework for thinking rigorously about agency, authorship, and accountability in the age of generative AI.

---

### Building

### Background

Classical charcoal artist. The studio practice came first. The theory followed from watching what the hand does that no other process replicates.

Working in the Hermetic tradition as transmitter and explicator.

Completing final quarter at The Evergreen State College. Beginning MA in Philosophy at Gonzaga University, fall 2026.
